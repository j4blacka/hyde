Helpful Information on Promoting Yourself and Your Art Online

In 2020, we created some resouces to help you promote yourself and your art online. In case you missed them, here there are:
  • Carolyn Cutshall conducted two webinars for a high-level view of online selling platforms and how to market them.
    • "Artist Workshop: Online Selling Platforms" on YouTube here: https://youtu.be/NBMQJIwUTzo
    • "Marketing Your On-line Selling Platform" on YouTube here: https://youtu.be/TBU44iexB1E
  • Amy Manson and Teresa Jardines conducted an online selling event and shared their lessons learned and G'Ann Zieger created a cheat sheet for a live sale. Get their PDF WLASTPromotionFaceBookLiveSalesEvents.pdf
  • G'Ann Zieger created a user guide to start selling on Etsy. Get her PDF OpenAnEtsyShopInAnHourOrLess.pdf (Note: the Table of Contents is clickable to navigate the guide.)
